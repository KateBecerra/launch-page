Local

Kate's cupcakes proudly uses all local ingredients provided by the wonderful town of Fort Bragg. We are lucky to live in a community with so much abundance and generosity. Not only do we locally source our ingredients but we also sell local. On Fridays 15% of our procedes from each purchase go back into our city park fund. Help support your local businesses and your city by shopping at Kate's Cupcakes.

Healthy

We use all the best ingredients in our baked goods. Each cupcake is made with organic ingredients and we even provide options for those who prefer gluten free and even sugar free treats! Just come on into the shop and ask for the special cakes. You can also order the Special cupcakes by the dozen just let us know in advance :)

Fun

At Kate's we proudly use our family recipe which has been passed down through generations, but we decided we wanted to share it with the world! That's why Kate's Cupcakes provides baking class for children ages 6-12 on every first Saturday of the month. No need to sign up just show up and we'll find you an apron!
